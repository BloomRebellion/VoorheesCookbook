Pineapple Chiffon Cheese Cake

No bake — chill several hours

Ingredients

Crust

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter or margarine
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts

Filling

  • 2 envelopes unflavored gelatin
  • 1/3 cup cold water
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 tablespoon grated lemon rind
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 1/3 cups crushed pineapple, drained
  • 2 egg whites
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 2 cups heavy cream, whipped

Directions

Combine crust ingredients. Press firmly against bottom and sides of a springform pan. Chill.

Soften gelatin in cold water. Combine next 4 ingredients (egg yolks, milk, lemon rind, lemon juice) in the top of a double boiler. Cook over hot water, stirring constantly, until custard coats a spoon. Add gelatin; stir until dissolved.

Chill until custard mounds slightly. Add crushed pineapple; mix well.

Beat egg whites until stiff with sugar. Fold gently into custard mixture with 2 cups whipped cream.

Pour into prepared crust. Chill several hours until firm.
