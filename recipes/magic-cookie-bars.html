Magic Cookie Bars

Yield: 54 bars, 2x1 inches · Oven 350°F

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups Kellogg's Corn Flake crumbs
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) regular margarine, melted
  • 1 cup coarsely chopped walnuts
  • 1 cup (6 oz. pkg.) semi-sweet chocolate morsels
  • 1 1/3 cups (3 1/2 oz. can) flaked coconut
  • 1 can Borden sweetened condensed milk (must be used)

Directions

Measure the corn flake crumbs, sugar, and margarine into a 13x9x2-inch baking pan; mix thoroughly. With the back of a tablespoon, press the mixture evenly and firmly in the bottom of the pan to form a crust.

Sprinkle the walnuts evenly over the crust. Scatter the chocolate morsels over the walnuts. Sprinkle the coconut evenly over the chocolate morsels. Pour the sweetened condensed milk evenly over the coconut.

Bake in a 350°F oven about 25 minutes, or until lightly browned around the edges. Cool. Cut into bars. Yield: 54 Magic Cookie Bars, 2x1 inches.
