Chocolate Coconut Supremes

Yields about 3 dozen pieces

Ingredients

  • 1 Hershey's giant milk chocolate bar with almonds
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 1 cup shredded coconut

Directions

Chop the Hershey's giant milk chocolate bar with almonds into small pieces. Melt the milk chocolate over hot, not boiling, water. Add the raisins and 1/2 cup of the shredded coconut and mix well.

Drop by teaspoonful onto a greased cookie sheet. Sprinkle the remaining 1/2 cup shredded coconut on top of the pieces. Chill in the refrigerator until firm. Yields about 3 dozen pieces.
