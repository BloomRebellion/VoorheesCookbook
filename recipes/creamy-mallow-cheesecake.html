Creamy Mallow Cheesecake

No-bake, chilled · Makes one 9-inch cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup vanilla wafer crumbs
  • 1/4 cup margarine, melted
  • 4 cups miniature marshmallows
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 (8 oz.) packages Philadelphia Brand cream cheese
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons lemon rind
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup heavy cream, whipped
  • Mandarin orange sections, for decorating (optional)

Directions

Combine the crumbs and margarine; reserve 1/4 cup for topping. Press the remaining crumb mixture onto the bottom of a 9-inch spring pan. Melt the marshmallows with the milk in a double boiler; stir until smooth. Chill until thickened.

Combine the softened cream cheese, lemon juice, lemon rind, and vanilla, beating until well blended and fluffy. Whip in the marshmallow mixture; fold in the whipped cream.

Pour into the crust; sprinkle with the reserved crumb mixture. Chill. If desired, decorate with mandarin orange sections.
