Self-Frosting Brownies

Makes 1 dozen brownies · Oven 350°F

Ingredients

  • 1 package (15 1/2 oz.) brownie mix
  • 1 cup miniature marshmallows
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate pieces
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°F. Prepare the mix according to the package directions. Spread evenly in a greased 8-inch square pan.

Sprinkle with the marshmallows, chocolate, and nuts. Bake for 30–35 minutes, or until done. Cool before cutting.
