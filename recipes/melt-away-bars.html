Melt Away Bars

Makes about 25 bars · Oven 350°F

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sifted all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup butter or margarine
  • 2 cans (1 lb. 5 oz. each) prepared peach pie filling
  • 2 egg whites
  • Dash of cream of tartar
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°F. Combine the flour, 1 cup sugar, egg yolk, and vanilla in a bowl. Cut in the butter with a pastry blender or 2 knives until the mixture resembles coarse cornmeal.

Turn into an ungreased jelly roll pan and pat into an even layer on the bottom and about 1 inch up the sides. Arrange the pie filling on the dough and set aside.

Combine the egg whites, cream of tartar, and 1/2 cup sugar. Beat until stiff peaks form when the beaters are lifted. Spread evenly over the peach filling and top with the walnuts.

Bake for 30–35 minutes, or until golden brown. Cool, then cut into bars.
