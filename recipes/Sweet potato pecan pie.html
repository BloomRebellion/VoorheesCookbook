Sweet Potato-Pecan Pie

Oven 400°F, then 350°F, 1 hour 15 minutes total

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mashed sweet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/8 teaspoon cloves
  • 3/4 cup dark corn syrup
  • 3 eggs, slightly beaten
  • 1 unbaked 9-inch pie shell
  • 3 tablespoons butter or margarine
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions

Preheat oven to 400°F. Combine all ingredients except eggs and pie shell in a saucepan. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, for 3–5 minutes until thickened.

Remove from heat. Add eggs. Mix well. Spoon into pie shell.

Bake 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 350°F and bake for 1 hour.

Makes one 9-inch pie.
