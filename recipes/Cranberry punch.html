Cranberry Punch

Heat to boiling, then chill

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (1 lb each) jellied cranberry sauce
  • 3 cups water
  • 3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/3 teaspoon allspice
  • 3/4 teaspoon cloves
  • 1 quart pineapple juice

Directions

Makes about 2 1/2 quarts. Crush cranberry sauce with a fork. Gradually add water. Beat with a rotary beater until smooth.

Combine next 7 ingredients in a saucepan. Stir in cranberry and pineapple juice. Heat to boiling point.

Cool and chill well. Pour over ice.
