Chews

Makes about 1 1/2 dozen · Oven 375°F

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mashed sweet potatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1 can (3 1/2 oz.) flaked coconut
  • 2 tablespoons butter or margarine, softened
  • 3/4 cup crushed pineapple, drained
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup crushed cornflakes

Directions

Preheat oven to 375°F. Combine all ingredients except the cornflakes in a bowl. Mix until blended. Shape into balls about the size of a walnut.

Roll the balls in the crushed cornflakes. Place on greased cookie sheets. Bake for 20 minutes, or until lightly browned.
