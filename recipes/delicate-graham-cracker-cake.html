Delicate Graham Cracker Cake

Makes two 8-inch layers · Oven 350°F

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sifted flour
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Approx. 1 1/4 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 eggs

Directions

Sift together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt; combine with the graham cracker crumbs. Place the shortening in a bowl. Add the dry ingredients, milk, and vanilla, and mix until the dry ingredients are dampened.

Beat 2 minutes with an electric mixer, or 300 strokes by hand. Pour into two greased, paper-lined 8-inch round cake pans. Bake in a moderate oven at 350°F for 25 to 30 minutes.

Cool. Fill and frost as desired.
