Breakfast Cookies

Oven 350°F, 13–15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsifted all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped cooked bacon
  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups grape-nut flakes

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°F. In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and soda. Mix well. Add the bacon, butter (or margarine), and eggs. Mix until blended. Stir in the cereal.

Drop by tablespoonfuls 2 inches apart onto a greased cookie sheet. Bake for 13–15 minutes, or until the cookies are lightly browned but still soft. Cool slightly before removing the cookies from the sheet.

Note: For freezer cookies, divide the dough in half. Place on wax paper and shape into rolls 2 inches in diameter. Wrap tightly and freeze. As needed, slice and bake as above.
