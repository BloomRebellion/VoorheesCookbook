Sweet Potato Pone

Oven 350°F, 1/2 hour

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sifted all-purpose flour
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon allspice
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/3 cup shortening
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 cups grated raw sweet potato

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°F. Sift flour, baking powder, salt, and spices into a bowl.

Cream shortening. Beat in sugar and eggs. Add dry ingredients with milk alternately. Beat until smooth. Add sweet potatoes. Mix well.

Bake in a greased 9-inch square pan 1/2 hour, or until done.

Makes 4–6 servings.
