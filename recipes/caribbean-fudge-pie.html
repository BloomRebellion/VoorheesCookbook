Caribbean Fudge Pie

Makes 8 servings · Oven 375°F

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 (11 1/2 oz.) package semi-sweet chocolate pieces, melted
  • 2 teaspoons instant coffee powder
  • 1 teaspoon rum extract
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 cup coarsely broken walnuts
  • 1/2 cup walnut halves for decoration
  • 1 9-inch unbaked pie shell

Directions

Cream the butter with the sugar, then beat in the eggs one at a time. Add the melted chocolate, instant coffee, and rum extract. Stir in the flour and broken walnuts, and turn into the pie shell.

Top with the remaining 1/2 cup walnut halves. Bake at 375°F for 25 minutes. Cool. Top with whipped cream, if desired. Makes eight servings.
