Orange Nut Coffee Cake

Oven 400°F, 25–30 minutes · Makes 1 layer

Ingredients

  • 2 cups biscuit mix
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Orange topping

  • 1/3 cup melted butter or margarine
  • 2/3 cup brown sugar, firmly packed
  • 1/2 cup grated orange rind
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/2 cup orange juice

Directions

Preheat oven to 400°F. Combine all the cake ingredients in a bowl and mix until blended. Spoon the mixture into a greased 8-inch round pan.

Bake for 25–30 minutes, or until the cake springs back when pressed lightly with a finger. Spread with the orange topping.

For the topping: Combine the butter (or margarine) and brown sugar. Spread over the cake. Sprinkle with the orange rind and walnuts, then pour the orange juice over all. Broil until bubbly. Makes 1 layer.
