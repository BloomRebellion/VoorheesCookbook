Coconut Squares

Makes 16 squares · Oven 350°F

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups sifted all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 1 cup well-drained crushed pineapple
  • 1 tablespoon softened butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 cups flaked coconut

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°F. Combine the flour and 1/4 cup sugar in a bowl. Cut in the shortening with a pastry blender or 2 knives until the mixture is fine. Pat into the bottom of a 9-inch square pan. Bake for 15 minutes.

Top with the pineapple. Combine the butter, 1/2 cup sugar, and egg. Beat well. Add the coconut and blend thoroughly. Spread over the pineapple.

Return to the oven and bake for 25–30 minutes, or until done. Cut into squares.
