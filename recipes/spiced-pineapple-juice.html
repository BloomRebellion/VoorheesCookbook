Spiced Pineapple Juice

Makes about 1 1/2 quarts

Ingredients

  • 1 can (1 qt. 14 oz.) pineapple juice
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/3 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1 stick cinnamon
  • 2 whole cloves
  • 1 whole allspice

Directions

Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and mix well. Bring to a boil over medium heat, then reduce heat and simmer 10–15 minutes. Strain. Serve hot or cold.
