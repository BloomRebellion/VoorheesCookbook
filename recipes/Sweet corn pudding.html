Sweet Corn Pudding

Oven 350°F, 35–40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 can (1 lb) cream style corn
  • 1 can (1 lb) lima beans
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter or margarine
  • 1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon grated onion
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/3 cup evaporated milk

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°F. Combine ingredients in order given. Spoon into a greased shallow baking dish.

Bake for 35–40 minutes. Garnish with parsley if desired.

Makes 4–6 servings.
