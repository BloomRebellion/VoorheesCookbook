Chocolate Glaze

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 squares (1 oz. each) unsweetened chocolate
  • 2 tablespoons boiling water
  • 1 cup sifted confectioners' sugar
  • Dash of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla

Directions

Melt the butter and chocolate. Stir in the boiling water, confectioners' sugar, salt, and vanilla. Beat until well blended.
