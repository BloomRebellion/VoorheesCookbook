Piquant Pecans

Oven 325°F, 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine
  • 2 teaspoons garlic salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 4 cups pecan halves

Directions

Preheat oven to 325°F. Melt butter or margarine in skillet. Add garlic salt, hot sauce, and Worcestershire. Mix well.

Add pecan halves. Stir to coat well. Arrange in a single layer in a large, shallow pan.

Bake 30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until deep brown. Remove from oven and drain on paper towels.

Makes 4 cups.
