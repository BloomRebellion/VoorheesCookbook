Pasta Salad

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • 1 can black olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup green olives, sliced
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 small package fresh mushrooms (or 1 can mushrooms)
  • Dash of pepper
  • Dash of garlic powder
  • Kraft House Italian dressing

Directions

Cook and drain the macaroni. Combine all ingredients and mix well.
