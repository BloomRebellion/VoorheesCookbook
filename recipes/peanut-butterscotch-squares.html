Peanut-Butterscotch Squares

Makes about 2 dozen · Oven 350°F

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine
  • 1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups sifted all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 2/3 cup light corn syrup
  • 1 package (6 oz.) butterscotch flavored pieces
  • 1/2 cup chunky style peanut butter
  • 2 cups corn flakes

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°F. Cream the butter (or margarine) and brown sugar until fluffy. Stir in the flour (the mixture will be crumbly). Press into the bottom of an ungreased 13x9x2-inch baking pan. Bake 15 minutes.

Combine the sugar and corn syrup in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, then remove from heat. Add the butterscotch pieces and peanut butter, and stir until smooth. Sift in the corn flakes and mix well.

Spread evenly over the baked layer. Cool thoroughly, then cut into squares.
