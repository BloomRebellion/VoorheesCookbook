Sweet Potato-Banana Pie

Oven 400°F, then 350°F, about 40–45 minutes total

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup mashed sweet potato
  • 3 bananas, mashed
  • 1/4 cup melted butter or margarine
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 unbaked 9-inch pie shell

Directions

Preheat oven to 400°F. Beat eggs and sugars until light. Add remaining ingredients except pie shell; mix until smooth.

Pour into pie shell. Bake 10 minutes at 400°F, then reduce heat to 350°F and bake 30–35 minutes more, or until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
