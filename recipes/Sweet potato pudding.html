Sweet Potato Pudding

Oven 350°F, 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine
  • 1 1/2 cups plus 2 tablespoons firmly packed brown sugar
  • 3 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 cup mashed sweet potatoes
  • 1/3 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 3 egg whites, stiffly beaten

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°F. Cream butter or margarine. Add sugar. Beat until smooth.

Add egg yolks and remaining ingredients, except egg whites. Mix until well blended. Fold in egg whites.

Spoon into a buttered 1 1/2-quart casserole. Bake for 45 minutes, or until a knife inserted in center comes out clean.

Makes 6 servings.
