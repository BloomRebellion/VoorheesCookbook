Fruit Punch

Makes 1 1/2 gallons

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 1/3 cup lemon juice
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 cup grape juice
  • 1 cup strong, cold tea
  • 1 can (46 oz.) grapefruit juice
  • 1 quart ginger ale

Directions

Combine the sugar and water. Stir until the sugar is dissolved. Add the remaining ingredients, except the ginger ale. Chill. Add the ginger ale before serving. Makes 1 1/2 gallons.
