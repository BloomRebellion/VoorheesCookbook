Cranberry-Orange Relish

No cooking — chill several hours

Ingredients

  • 4 cups fresh cranberries
  • 1 medium orange
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts

Directions

Wash cranberries. Drain well. Grate orange peel; set aside. Peel and quarter orange.

Put orange and cranberries through the coarse blade of a food chopper. Add sugar, nuts, and grated peel. Mix well.

Chill for several hours before serving.

Makes about 4 cups.
