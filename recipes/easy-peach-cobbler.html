Easy Peach Cobbler

Makes 4–6 servings · Oven 350°F

Ingredients

  • 1 can (1 lb. 13 oz.) peach slices
  • 1/4 cup butter or margarine
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup biscuit mix

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°F. Drain the peaches, reserving the syrup. Melt the butter (or margarine) in a 9-inch square pan.

Combine the sugar, milk, vanilla, biscuit mix, and 2/3 cup of the reserved syrup. Mix well. Spoon the batter over the butter, then arrange the peach slices on top of the batter.

Bake 25–30 minutes.
