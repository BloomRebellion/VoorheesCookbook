Mexican Coffee

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 1/3 cup dark brown sugar, firmly packed
  • 2 (3-inch) cinnamon sticks
  • 1/2 cup ground coffee

Directions

In a medium saucepan, combine the water, sugar, and cinnamon. Bring to boiling, stirring until the sugar is dissolved. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, about 5 minutes.

Stir in the coffee; simmer, uncovered, 2 minutes. Remove from heat. Stir, cover, and let stand in a warm place until the coffee grounds settle, about 5 minutes. Carefully pour into a heated coffee pot or cups.
