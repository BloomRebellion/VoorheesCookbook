Dairy Salad Dressing

No cooking — chill until ready to serve

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (1/2 pint) creamed cottage cheese
  • 1 cup (1/2 pint) dairy sour cream
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk

Directions

Combine cottage cheese, sour cream, and buttermilk. Mix well.

Chill until ready to serve.

Yield: about 2 1/3 cups dressing.
