Candied Sweet Potato Casserole

Makes 10 servings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar, divided
  • 2 tablespoons orange juice
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice, divided
  • 3 pounds sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 4 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  • 2 cups miniature marshmallows

Directions

Heat oven to 375°F. Mix 1/4 cup of the brown sugar, orange juice, vanilla, and 1 teaspoon of the pumpkin pie spice in a large bowl. Add sweet potatoes and toss to coat. Spoon into a 13x9-inch baking dish. Dot with 2 tablespoons of the butter, cover, and bake 45 minutes or until potatoes are tender.

Meanwhile, mix the flour, remaining 1/4 cup brown sugar, and remaining 1/2 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice in a medium bowl. Cut in the remaining 2 tablespoons butter with a fork until crumbly, then stir in the pecans and marshmallows.

Remove the casserole from the oven, uncover, and stir gently to coat the potatoes evenly. Sprinkle with the topping and bake uncovered 7 to 10 minutes, until the pecans and topping are golden brown.
