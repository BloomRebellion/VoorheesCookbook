Quick Corn Pudding

Oven 350°F, 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 can (1 lb) cream style corn
  • 1 can (1 lb) whole kernel corn, drained
  • 1 egg, slightly beaten
  • 1/3 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°F. In a bowl, combine all ingredients. Mix well.

Spoon into a well-greased 1 1/2-quart casserole. Bake 40 minutes, or until firm.

Makes 4 servings.
