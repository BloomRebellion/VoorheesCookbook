Peanut Butter Cream Pie

Makes one 8-inch pie

Ingredients

  • 1 baked pie shell
  • 1 package (3 5/8 oz.) vanilla pudding and pie filling
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1 cup miniature marshmallows

Directions

In a saucepan, combine the pudding, peanut butter, and milk. Mix until well blended. Cook over medium heat for 10 minutes, or until thick and smooth.

Pour into the baked pie shell. Top with the marshmallows. Broil for 5 minutes, or until golden brown.
