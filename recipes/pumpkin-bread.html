Pumpkin Bread

Bake at 350°F for 45–60 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/3 cup melted oleo (butter)
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1 cup pumpkin
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp. cloves
  • 1 tsp. soda
  • 3/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. baking powder

Directions

Combine all ingredients and mix well. Pour into a greased loaf pan. Bake at 350°F for 45–60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
