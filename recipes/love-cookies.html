Love Cookies

Makes about 2 dozen · Oven 325°F

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine
  • 1 cup seedless raisins
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 cup hot milk
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter or margarine
  • 1 cup chopped pecans
  • 1 1/2 cups sifted confectioners' sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, sifted

Directions

Preheat oven to 325°F. Combine the sugar, cinnamon, 1/2 cup butter, raisins, and 1 cup water in a pan. Mix well. Combine the baking soda and remaining tablespoon of water. Stir until the soda is dissolved. Add to the raisin mixture and mix well. Set aside to cool.

Sift the flour and baking powder into the cooled mixture. Beat well. Spread the mixture on a greased cookie sheet. Bake for 30 minutes, or until done.

Combine the remaining ingredients (hot milk, melted butter, chopped pecans, and confectioners' sugar) and mix well. Spread over the warm cake. Cool. Cut into squares.
