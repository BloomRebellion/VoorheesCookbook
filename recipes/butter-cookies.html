Butter Cookies

Makes about 6 1/2 dozen · Oven 325°F

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup butter or margarine, softened
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 tablespoon light cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups unsifted all-purpose flour
  • Confectioners' sugar, for decorating
  • Chopped walnuts, pistachios, pecans, or almonds, for decorating

Directions

In a large bowl, with an electric mixer on medium speed, beat the butter, sugar, and salt until light. Add the egg yolks, cream, and vanilla. Gradually add the flour, stirring with a wooden spoon until the dough is smooth.

Shape into a ball, flatten, and cut into thirds. Wrap each third in waxed paper. Refrigerate until firm, about 2 hours.

Preheat oven to 325°F. Roll out one third of the dough to about 1/4 inch thickness. Cut with a cookie cutter, or cut half moons. Place 1 inch apart on ungreased cookie sheets. Sprinkle some with chopped nuts. Bake 12 to 15 minutes. Let cool.

Roll out the second third of dough. Cut with a diamond-shape cutter and crescent-shape cutter. Decorate some, and do the rest in strips. Sprinkle with nuts. Makes about 6 1/2 dozen.
