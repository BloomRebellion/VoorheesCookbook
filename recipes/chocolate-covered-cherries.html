Chocolate Covered Cherries

Makes about 3 dozen

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup butter or margarine
  • 2 cups marshmallow fluff
  • Dash of salt
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 4 cups sifted confectioners' sugar
  • 36 maraschino cherries
  • 1 package (12 oz.) semi-sweet chocolate pieces

Directions

Cream the butter or margarine. Beat in the marshmallow fluff, salt, and almond extract. Add the confectioners' sugar gradually, mixing well after each addition. Turn out and knead until smooth, working in extra sugar if the mixture becomes too sticky. Refrigerate 1 hour.

Drain the cherries thoroughly. Wrap each cherry in the fondant, being sure it's completely covered. Refrigerate.

Melt the chocolate in the top part of a double boiler over hot, not boiling, water. Dip the cherries in the melted chocolate. Place on waxed paper and refrigerate to harden the chocolate.
