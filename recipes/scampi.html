Scampi

Ingredients

  • 12 jumbo shrimp, raw, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon finely minced shallots
  • 2 teaspoons crushed garlic
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon fresh basil, chopped
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

In a large skillet, melt butter over low flame. Sauté shallots and garlic until clear and you can smell them. Add shrimp and increase to medium flame. Cook 2 minutes, then add tomatoes and basil. Reduce to low heat and simmer until the shrimp is firm to the touch, about 3 to 4 minutes.

Spread the tomato sauce over crostini or toast and place shrimp on top. Serve warm and enjoy.
