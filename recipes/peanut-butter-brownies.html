Peanut Butter Brownies

Makes 16 brownies · Oven 350°F

Ingredients

  • 2 squares unsweetened chocolate
  • 1/4 cup butter or margarine
  • 1/3 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 2/3 cup sifted all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup coarsely chopped salted peanuts

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°F. Lightly grease an 8x8x2-inch baking pan. Melt the chocolate, butter, and peanut butter over hot, not boiling, water. Set aside. Sift the flour with the baking powder. Set aside.

In a large bowl, with a wooden spoon or portable electric mixer at medium speed, beat the eggs and sugar until light. Beat in the chocolate mixture and vanilla. Stir in the flour mixture and chopped peanuts.

Spread the mixture evenly in the prepared pan. Bake 25–30 minutes. Let the brownies cool slightly in the pan on a wire rack. While still warm, cut into 2-inch squares with a sharp knife. Then let cool completely. Makes 16 brownies.
