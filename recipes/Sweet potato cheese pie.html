Sweet Potato Cheese Pie

Oven 350°F, 8–10 minutes, then chill until firm

Ingredients

  • 1 cup vanilla wafer crumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter or margarine
  • 4 cups miniature marshmallows
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup mashed sweet potatoes
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/4 teaspoon cloves
  • 1 cup heavy cream, whipped

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°F. Combine crumbs and butter. Press onto bottom of a 9-inch pie pan. Bake for 8–10 minutes. Cool.

Melt marshmallows with milk in top part of a double boiler. Stir until dissolved. Chill until mixture mounds slightly.

Add sweet potato, orange juice, vanilla, and spices. Mix well. Beat until smooth with rest of sugar. Fold in whipped cream.

Spoon into pie shell. Chill well until firm.
