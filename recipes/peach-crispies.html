Peach Crispies

Makes 4–6 servings · Broiled

Ingredients

  • 6 large canned peach halves
  • 1/4 cup peach syrup
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1/4 cup enriched corn flakes
  • 1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg

Directions

Place the peach halves in a broiler pan, hollow side up. Combine the remaining ingredients. Spoon the mixture into the peach halves.

Broil about 4 inches away from the heat until browned, watching carefully.
